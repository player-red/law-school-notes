Facts:
- Appellant stole a gas meter and fractured a gas pipe.
- Coal gas escaped and endangered W who was asleep and inhaled the gas. 
- Convicted for endangering W's life maliciously.

Arguments
Appellants:
- Acting maliciously means [[Y1 T3 Criminal Law I/CONCEPTS/Mens rea\|Mens rea]] should have existed, which did not exist in this case. Mens rea -> intention to do harm or at least foresee harm and commit the act anyway

Court's decision:
- Not necessary to prove that he intended to do it, he had sufficient knowledge that it might happen as a consequence of his actions + what he did was done unlawfully and maliciously.
- Even if he did not intend the injury to W, he foresaw that removal of the gas meter might cause injury to someone.
