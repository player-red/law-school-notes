By [[Y1 T2 Economy/PEOPLE/Adam Smith\|Adam Smith]].
The theory that states that man is driven by self-interest (an invisible hand that guides him). He believes that this pursuit of self-interest more often than not leads to actions that act in favour of public interest. He uses this basis to argue that governments should be using a laissez-faire approach to regulation and governance of the market, and advocates a free-market economy.
