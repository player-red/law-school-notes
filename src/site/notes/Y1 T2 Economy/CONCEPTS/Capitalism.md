An economic system based on the private ownership of the means of production ([[Y1 T2 Power/CONCEPTS/Private Property\|Private Property]]) to obtain maximum amounts of profit. 

[[ARCHIVE/Y1/Y1 T1/Y1 T1 Society/PEOPLE/Karl Marx\|Karl Marx]] was a staunch opponent of it, explaining how it led to simplification of class relations to monetary relationships and exploitation of the working class (proletariat) in his work [[ARCHIVE/Y1/Y1 T1/Y1 T1 Society/READINGS/PRIMARY/SOC2.05 Marx & Engels, The Communist Manifesto\|SOC2.05 Marx & Engels, The Communist Manifesto]].
