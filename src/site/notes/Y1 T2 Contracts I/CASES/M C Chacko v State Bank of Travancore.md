tags: #consideration 

Facts:
- Plaintiff (State Bank of Travancore) advanced a loan to High Land Bank, of which the defendant (MC Chacko) was the manager.
- Guaranteed by KC Chacko, defendant’s father.
- When High Land failed to repay, the plaintiff filed a suit against HL Bank and KC Chacko, and MC Chacko’s two siblings and mother because KC Chacko transferred all assets to them under a family settlement deed. 
- Family settlement deed said that KC did not have any outstanding debts, but had guaranteed his son’s repayment of the loan he had taken. 

Decision:
- Trial court: decided against MC Chacko
- High court: agreed with Trial court.
- Supreme Court: says that there was a strict [[Y1 T2 Contracts I/CONCEPTS/Privity\|Privity]] requirement, decided in favour of MC Chacko.
