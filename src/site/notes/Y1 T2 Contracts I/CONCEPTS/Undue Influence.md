/Sec 19A of [[Y1 T2 Contracts I/READINGS/Indian Contract Act, 1872\|Indian Contract Act, 1872]]: 
- Power to set aside contract due to undue influence.
- Court has the power to set it aside absolutely or to set terms and conditions if the party entitled to avoid it received any benefit from it.
