/- Required to form a valid contract. 
- [[Y1 T2 Contracts I/CONCEPTS/Acceptance\|Acceptance]] by the offeree is an expression of consent and leads to the formation of a valid contract.
- If consent is obtained under duress, the contract is voidable at the option of the party who was under duress.
