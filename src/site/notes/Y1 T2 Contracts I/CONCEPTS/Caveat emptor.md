Meaning "buyer beware". Used as a disclaimer of warranties as a way to balance the information asymmetry between buyer and seller, since the buyer may not be aware of defects in the product that the seller might. 
Often balanced with [[Y1 T2 Contracts I/CONCEPTS/Consensus ad idem\|Consensus ad idem]].
