- Must be absolute and unqualified. 
- Must be in mirror image of the offer
	- To prevent misunderstandings involving counteroffers
- Must be an expression of assent, ie cannot be silent. 
	- Can be accepted by performing conditions.
- Must be made in response to the offer. 
- A contract can be accepted by performance as well. 
- When offers are made and accepted by telephone, the place where the acceptance is heard by the offeror is the place where the contract is made.
