Statements that are ==factually== false.
Section 18 of the [[Y1 T2 Contracts I/READINGS/Indian Contract Act, 1872\|Indian Contract Act, 1872]]:
- Positive assertion that is untrue though the maker of the statement believes it is true.
- A breach of duty without intent to deceive, gives an advantage to the person committing it.
- Causing a party to an agreement to make a mistake as to the substance of the contract.
