- The purpose and design of an agreement.
- Both parties must have intended it. 
- Different from [[Y1 T2 Contracts I/CONCEPTS/Motive\|Motive]]. For example, the object of a contract may be to borrow money, but the motive behind borrowing this money might be to perform an illegal act. 
- Sec. 24: if [[Y1 T2 Contracts I/CONCEPTS/Consideration\|Consideration]]s/objects are unlawful in any part, the agreement is void.
