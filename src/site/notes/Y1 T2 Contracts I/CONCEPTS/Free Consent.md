[[Y1 T2 Contracts I/READINGS/Indian Contract Act, 1872\|Indian Contract Act, 1872]] Sec. 13 - Definition of consent
Two or more people are said to consent when they agree upon the same thing in the same sense.

Sec. 14 - Definition of free consent 
Free when not caused by 
- [[Y1 T2 Contracts I/CONCEPTS/Coercion\|Coercion]]
- Undue influence
- Fraud
- Misrepresentation
- Mistake
