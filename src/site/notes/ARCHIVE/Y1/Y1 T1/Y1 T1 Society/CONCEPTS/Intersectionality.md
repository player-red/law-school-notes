Coined by Kimberly Crenshaw.

The concept in which multiple different identities ([[ARCHIVE/Y1/Y1 T1/Y1 T1 Society/CONCEPTS/Gender\|Gender]], sex, religion, caste, race, etc) come together to shape an individual's place in society, which privileges they have and how they are oppressed.
