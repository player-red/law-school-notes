- Legally recognized union between spouses.
- One of the very few concepts that is found in very similar forms throughout cultures. 
- Socially sanctions the sexual activity and reproduction between two people. 
- Forms an important part of [[ARCHIVE/Y1/Y1 T1/Y1 T1 Society/CONCEPTS/Kinship\|Kinship]] relations, explains [[ARCHIVE/Y1/Y1 T1/Y1 T1 Society/CONCEPTS/Family\|Family]] structures, [[ARCHIVE/Y1/Y1 T1/Y1 T1 Society/CONCEPTS/Gender\|Gender]] roles, forms the basis for [[ARCHIVE/Y1/Y1 T1/Y1 T1 Society/CONCEPTS/Domestic labour\|Domestic labour]].
